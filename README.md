# enterprise-ai-platform
Enterprise AI Platform implementing RAG, MLOps, Agentic AI, Azure, AWS, DevSecOps and Kubernetes with production-ready architecture, CI/CD pipelines, documentation and real-world enterprise use cases.
There is no single "best" AI, as each excels at different tasks. For general versatility, use ChatGPT; for deep thinking and writing, use Claude; for real-time cited research, use Perplexity; and choose Copilot or Gemini if your workflow is tied to Microsoft or Google ecosystems. [1, 2, 3, 4]  
Here is how each tool specifically compares: 

• ChatGPT: The best all-rounder. It is ideal for creative writing, brainstorming, coding assistance, and image generation. It has the widest ecosystem of features, including web browsing, a conversational voice mode, and data analysis. 
• Claude: The best for complex reasoning and handling long documents. It is known for having a less "robotic" and more human-like tone, making it highly preferred for editing, legal analysis, and sensitive knowledge work. 
• Perplexity: The best search and fact-checking engine. Instead of just giving an answer, it natively scours the live web and provides clickable citations so you can verify the sources yourself. 
• Gemini: The best for Google Workspace integration. If your day-to-day work lives in Gmail, Google Docs, or Google Sheets, Gemini is highly efficient for pulling data directly from your personal cloud without copying and pasting. 
• Copilot: The best for Microsoft 365 and developers. It is deeply integrated into Outlook, Word, and Teams for business productivity, and uses OpenAI's high-end models for coding. [1, 4, 6]
