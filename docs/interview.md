# Interview Questions

## What are Embeddings?

Embeddings convert text into numerical vectors preserving semantic meaning.

---

## Why do we need ChromaDB?

To efficiently store and search vector embeddings.

---

## Why not SQL?

SQL performs keyword matching.

Vector databases perform semantic similarity search.

---

## Why Sentence Transformers?

To generate high-quality embeddings.

---
